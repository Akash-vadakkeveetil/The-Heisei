    Project Name: HEISEI: Streamlining Disaster Relief with Real-Time Coordination

    Guided By: Asst. Prof. Amrutha B K

    Group No.: 11

    Team Members: Ajeeb Kunhuty (TLY21CS008), Akash V V (TLY21CS009), Akhil K K (TLY21CS010), Ashwin Nambiar (TLY21CS019)

    Natural disasters, such as floods, earthquakes, hurricanes, and wildfires, test the resilience and preparedness of nations, often exposing gaps in the ability to provide timely and appropriate relief. Heisei enhances disaster relief by connecting donors, volunteers, and relief camps in real-time, ensuring essential resources reach those in need.

    Key Features

    Transparent Donation Process: Donors can view inventory levels and send items directly to volunteers.

    Real-Time Coordination: Track resource availability at each relief center, ensuring donations match current demands.

    Blockchain Security: Transactions are securely recorded on an immutable blockchain ledger, ensuring transparency and trust. This technology ensures data integrity, prevents unauthorized access, and builds confidence among all stakeholders.

    Volunteer Network: Volunteers collect and deliver resources using optimized routes, reducing transportation time and costs.

    By integrating real-time data, automated processes, and blockchain technology, Heisei streamlines disaster relief, ensuring that the right resources reach the right places at the right time. This comprehensive framework contributes to more resilient and well-supported communities during times of crisis.
